TEXTMOD

Textmod is an extension built for discord. You can run commands and modify your text in ways that normal users can't! You can run (for example) "-long Hello World" right in discord and it would send "H e l l o W o r l d" instead! Another cool thing about Textmod is its ability to generate image commands, use free custom emoji, and even allow custom rich presence which will spice up your profile! This is my most popular application by far, and version 2.0 is on its way.

Join my discord to get updates on TextMod's development!
